Classic Series

Our Classic Series is a reliable in-stock cabinetry system with timeless finishes and consistent sizing, built to support both everyday projects and semi-custom upgrades.

Delight Series

The Delight Series offers a step up in both finish and design, with a wider selection of colors and materials to suit more refined interiors. It brings added flexibility while maintaining the efficiency and consistency of our core cabinet system.

Grand Series

The Grand Series focuses on more modern, design-forward finishes, including a curated selection from AGT and EGGER. It expands the palette with richer textures and contemporary tones, giving designers more freedom to create distinctive spaces.

Luxury Series

New in 2026, the Luxury Series represents our most elevated offering we have so far, featuring advanced lacquer finishes and the new Slim Shaker profile. With refined details and a more sophisticated look, it is designed for projects that demand a higher level of finish and visual impact.

Our Story

Frameless reinvented for Canadian homes

GoldenHome has been delivering high-quality cabinetry solutions since 1999, building a strong global foundation across multiple markets. Today, GoldenHome Cabinetry Eastern Canada brings that experience closer to home through a growing local manufacturing and supply network based in Ontario.

With integrated warehousing and local production capabilities, we are able to support projects with greater speed, flexibility, and consistency. From in-stock cabinetry to locally customized solutions, our approach is designed to meet the needs of contractors, designers, and homeowners across Canada.
